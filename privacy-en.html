Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you apply for a Malta Seaman’s Book through our website.

1. Data We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Passport details
  • Seafarer certificates (if provided)
  • Contract with shipping company (if available)
  • Photograph

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your application for the Malta Seaman’s Book
  • To communicate with you regarding your application
  • To deliver the final document via courier
  • To comply with legal and maritime regulations

3. Data Storage & Protection

We store your data securely and only for the duration necessary to process your application. We do not share your data with third parties except for official maritime authorities and courier services involved in delivery.

4. Your Rights

  • You may request access to your data
  • You may request correction or deletion of your data
  • You may withdraw consent at any time

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@maltaseaman.com
